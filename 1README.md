## 📝 Task 5 Project Report (Self-Reflection)

While researching UPI fraud for this task, the detail that surprised me the most was the intense psychological manipulation involved in modern scams. Fraudsters rarely rely on complex hacking; instead, they exploit human emotions like urgency, greed, or fear. The "Receive Money" scam is a perfect example, relying entirely on the victim's distraction rather than breaking through bank security. It was alarming to learn how easily educated college students fall for task-based Telegram scams simply because the scammers build a false sense of trust first.

As a direct result of completing this module, the one digital habit I will permanently change is how I handle peer-to-peer transactions. I will never enter my UPI PIN when interacting with an unknown buyer online, and I will start using a secondary bank account with limited funds for my daily campus expenses to minimize financial risk.
